## Why fish oil in the first place

A year of bloodwork showed my omega-3 index sitting uncomfortably low. Increasing fatty fish intake wasn't going to happen reliably, so supplementation made sense.

## Brand choice

Fish oil quality varies wildly. Cheap brands tend to be oxidized by the time they reach your shelf, which is worse than not taking any. Nordic Naturals publishes third-party oxidation results and has the credibility I was looking for.

## Experience

Two softgels a day, with a meal. No aftertaste. No "fish burps" — the thing that made me quit the $12 Costco bottle within a week. Three months in, retested, and my omega-3 index was in the normal range.

## Verdict

I don't feel anything from this day-to-day, which is what you want from a long-term health supplement.
