## Why I tried it

Podcast-pushed curiosity. Enough people in my feed were mentioning it that I wanted to form my own opinion.

## Thirty-day trial

Two capsules each morning with breakfast for thirty days. I kept a focus log — rating from 1 to 10 across three daily check-ins — for the duration.

## Results

No meaningful change versus my baseline. The first three days had some placebo shine; that faded.

## The real issue

Alpha Brain uses a proprietary blend, which means you can't see how much of each active ingredient you're actually getting. Given the pricing, my best guess is that most of the actives are underdosed below the clinical research thresholds. At $60 for 30 servings, I'd rather buy Alpha-GPC and L-tyrosine separately at proper doses for a quarter of the price.

## Verdict

Skip. Buy individual nootropics if you're curious, or save your money entirely.
