## Why bone-specific

I lift weights and do a fair amount of bodyweight work, and bone density is something you don't notice until you do, at which point the deficit is usually decades old. A combined Ca + D3 + K2 + Mg formula is the cleanest way to cover the bases without juggling four bottles.

## Why these four together

Calcium without vitamin K2 has been associated with calcium ending up in arteries instead of bones, which is the exact opposite of what you want. K2 directs calcium to bone tissue. D3 helps with absorption. Magnesium is a cofactor for vitamin D activation. Skip any one of them and the others work less well.

## Routine

Two capsules with any meal that has real fat in it. D3 and K2 are fat-soluble, so dry-stomach dosing is mostly wasted, and the bigger the fat load in the meal, the better the absorption.
