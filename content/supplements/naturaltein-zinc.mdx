## Why zinc

Zinc is a workhorse mineral, immune function, wound healing, testosterone, skin turnover, and Indian diets are commonly low on it because the staple grains are high in phytates that block absorption. Topping it up daily covers ground a lot of multivitamins underdose.

## Routine

One capsule with a fatty meal alongside the rest of the daily stack. I'm not stacking it on top of a higher-dose calcium serving in the same swallow because the two minerals fight each other for absorption.
