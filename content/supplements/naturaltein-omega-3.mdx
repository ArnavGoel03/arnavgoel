## Why I take omega-3

EPA and DHA have the best-supported evidence base of any single supplement for inflammation, cognition, and joint comfort. It's been a daily non-negotiable for me for years.

## Why this one

A lot of fish oils fail the basic smell-test, you burp them for an hour after taking them. That's almost always an oxidation issue; the capsules went rancid in warehouse storage. This one doesn't do that, which is the only thing I really need from a fish oil.

## Routine

Two capsules with breakfast. Fat-soluble actives absorb better with food. I don't cycle it.
