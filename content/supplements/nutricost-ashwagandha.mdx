## What ashwagandha actually does

The clinical literature is decent: modest reductions in cortisol, subjective stress, and sleep onset over 4-8 weeks. Not a sedative, not an instant-effect compound, more of a slowly-shifting baseline.

## Why this one

Cheapest way to find out if ashwagandha works for you specifically. If it does, the question is whether the slightly more expensive KSM-66 or Sensoril extracts are worth the upgrade. If it doesn't do anything, you've spent fifteen dollars finding out.

## Routine

One capsule in the morning with food. I don't take it in the evening because for some people it's mildly stimulating; better to find out in the daytime.
