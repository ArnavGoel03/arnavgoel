## Why I started

I was averaging 71% sleep quality on my Whoop over the prior three months and getting through coffee faster than I wanted to. A friend who's a PA kept mentioning magnesium glycinate for sleep, so I finally tried it.

## Protocol

Two capsules (240mg elemental magnesium) about an hour before bed. No food requirement.

## What changed

Week one: I fell asleep faster. Not dramatically — maybe ten minutes off sleep latency — but consistently. By week three my Whoop average was 84%, and my REM percentages were the highest I'd ever tracked. I can't claim any of this is solely magnesium, but the supplement is the only variable that changed.

## Why bisglycinate specifically

Magnesium citrate works but sends most people running for the bathroom. Oxide is cheap but poorly absorbed. Bisglycinate is the well-tolerated, well-absorbed middle ground, and Thorne's is third-party tested.

## Verdict

Of every supplement I've tried, this is the one I'd miss most if I ran out.
