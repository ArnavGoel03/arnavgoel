## Why Gold Standard is the default

Gold Standard is the most-reviewed whey protein on Amazon across every major market (US, UK, India). That popularity isn't an accident:

- The primary ingredient is **whey isolate**, not concentrate, higher protein-per-scoop than most mid-tier proteins at similar price
- The formula adds **whey peptides** (partially hydrolyzed whey) for slightly faster absorption
- It's been on shelves since the late 1990s and is **banned-substance tested** (Informed Choice-style verification)

## Dose

One scoop (~30 g) delivers 24 g protein. The cookies-and-cream flavor has cocoa powder and actual biscuit pieces, so the macros per scoop come in around ~120 calories, standard for a flavored whey blend.

## When flavor matters

Unflavored whey wins on versatility. Flavored whey wins on the "can I drink this from a shaker with just water" test. Cookies-and-cream is the safest flavor bet, inoffensive, mixes clean, doesn't go medicinal the way strawberry often does.

Unusual among proteins, Gold Standard is genuinely stocked everywhere: amazon.com, amazon.in, amazon.co.uk. Not all SKUs match across regions (flavors and sizes vary), so confirm the exact variant before buying internationally.
