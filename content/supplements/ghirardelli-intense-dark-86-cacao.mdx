Filed under supplements, not snacks. 86% cacao bar, four servings per 3.17 oz, ~6g of sugar in the whole bar. The supplement case for very-dark chocolate is the cocoa flavanols (epicatechin) plus a meaningful magnesium hit per ounce, both backed by published cardiovascular and cognitive endpoints.

The eat-it-as-a-supplement framing only works above ~85% cacao. Below that the sugar load swallows the polyphenol benefit. 86% sits in the sweet spot: bitter enough that one square is genuinely satisfying, sweet enough that it does not feel like medicine.

## Routine

One square (about 4g) after dinner most nights. Pairs with the espresso routine I run mornings, so the bar lasts a week without becoming a thing I overeat.
