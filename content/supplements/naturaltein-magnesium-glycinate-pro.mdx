## Why magnesium

Most adults are at least mildly deficient and the symptoms are easy to miss, restless sleep, tight calves, an overall low-grade wired-but-tired feeling. Glycinate is the form I went with because it doesn't cause the GI problems of magnesium oxide or citrate at the doses I want to take.

## Routine

One capsule about thirty minutes before bed. I noticed a real difference in how quickly I fell asleep within the first week.

## What I like about Naturaltein's version

Single ingredient, sensible dose, no proprietary blends or filler stack. The "Pro" label seems to just mean a higher dose per capsule.
