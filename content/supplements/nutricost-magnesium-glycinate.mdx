## Why this one

I cycled through more expensive magnesium brands and kept landing back on Nutricost. Same active form, fraction of the price, and the bigger 180-cap bottle means I'm not reordering every six weeks.

## Routine

Two capsules about thirty minutes before bed. The glycinate form is what makes this work for daily use, citrate at the same dose would send me to the bathroom.

## What you give up vs the premium brands

Mostly nothing measurable. The premium brands have nicer labels, sometimes vegan capsules, and occasionally a slight purity certification advantage. None of those changed how I sleep.
