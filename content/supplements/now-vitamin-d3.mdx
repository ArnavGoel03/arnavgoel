## The case for testing first

Vitamin D is one of the few supplements where *more is not better*. If you're already optimal, adding more doesn't help. If you're deficient, the improvement can be significant — better mood, fewer colds, improved recovery. Get a 25-OH vitamin D blood test before you start.

## My numbers

I tested at 22 ng/mL in winter, which is low. Started one softgel per day. Retested four months later at 48 ng/mL — squarely in the optimal range.

## Why NOW

Cheapest reputable brand I've found. Most D3 softgels are interchangeable; the brand matters less here than with fish oil.

## Consider pairing with K2

Higher-dose D3 is often paired with vitamin K2 (MK-7) for calcium metabolism reasons. This product doesn't include K2 — you'd buy it separately.

## Verdict

Not exciting, but exactly the kind of unglamorous supplement that actually changes your labs.
