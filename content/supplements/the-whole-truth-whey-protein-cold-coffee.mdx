## Why this one

The Whole Truth is one of the few Indian protein brands whose label genuinely matches their marketing. Concentrate from grass-fed European whey, real cocoa, real coffee, stevia for sweetness, no maltodextrin or sucralose. The cold coffee SKU is the one I rotate into the morning shake when I'm not reaching for unflavored.

## Dose

One ~30 g scoop in cold milk or water gets me ~24 g protein and a usable shot of caffeine, which is why it lands in the morning slot specifically. Mixes cleanly without a blender, foam settles in a minute.

## Brand context

Indian D2C, transparent ingredient sourcing, sells direct and through Amazon India at near-retail. Pricier per gram than Myprotein's Impact Whey but I'll pay the premium for a label I don't have to second-guess.
