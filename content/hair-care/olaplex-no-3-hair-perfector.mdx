Olaplex's at-home pre-shampoo treatment, applied to damp hair, left for 10 to 30 minutes, rinsed, then shampooed and conditioned. Same active as the No. 5 conditioner, just at higher concentration and longer dwell time, which is the bit that justifies the extra step.

I run it once a week, the same day I cycle the deep conditioner. It is not a quick fix; the value is supposed to compound over weeks of consistent use rather than one dramatic wash.
