Bumble and bumble's original Surf Spray, the small 1.7 fl oz bottle. The pitch is "wave and texture in a bottle." The reality is mineral-salt spray that gives air-dried hair a matte, slightly gritty finish without the crunchy hold of a gel.

Spray on towel-dried hair, scrunch, air-dry. Works on the days I want loose volume without product weight. Does not work as a styling primer for blow-dries: the salt drags the brush.

The price-per-ounce is steep for what amounts to magnesium and seaweed in water. The reason it stays in rotation is the smell, a coconut-pineapple-vacation note that no drugstore alternative I have tried matches.
