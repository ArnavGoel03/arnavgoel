The shampoo half of the Olaplex bond-builder line, same active (bis-aminopropyl diglycol dimaleate) as the No. 3 pre-shampoo and No. 5 conditioner, just dosed for a wash-and-rinse step instead of a soak. Cleansing is mid by design; this is not the bottle that strips an oily scalp clean.

In the current rotation it sits between the No. 3 pre-shampoo treatment and the No. 5 conditioner on bond-repair days, and rotates with the 2% ketoconazole shampoo on the days I am running an antifungal cycle for scalp flaking. Verdict pending real notes after a full bottle.
