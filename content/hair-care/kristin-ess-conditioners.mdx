Two formulations from Kristin Ess, sulfate-free and color-safe, picked up at Target. The Palo Santo Softening + Moisturizing variant is the everyday lightweight one; the Signature is the heavier moisturizing variant aimed at drier hair. I rotate between the two depending on whether the ends feel dry or weighed down.

One card because the brand line and the format are the same; only the scent and the moisture weight changes between bottles. Verdict on which one stays in the shower, plus how they handle hard-water build-up, pending real notes after a full bottle of each.
