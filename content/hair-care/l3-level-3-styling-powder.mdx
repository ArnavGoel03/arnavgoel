L3 Level 3 Styling Powder, the matte volumizing powder marketed as a no-residue alternative to wax. Sprinkle a pinch into the roots, work it in with the fingers, and the hair holds shape for the day without the helmet feeling pomade leaves behind.

Works as a baseline volumizer for finer hair days. Not the best in the powder category I have tried (Toppik FiberHold and Got2b Powder'ful both hold harder), but priced low enough that it earns the rotation slot rather than the toss pile.
