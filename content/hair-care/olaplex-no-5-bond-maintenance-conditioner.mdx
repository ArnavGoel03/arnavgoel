The in-shower conditioner from Olaplex's bond-repair lineup, the active is bis-aminopropyl diglycol dimaleate (the same ingredient under the No. 0 / No. 3 hood). The 100 ml travel size lets you sanity-check whether it does anything for your hair before committing to the larger bottle.

I run it after the No. 3 Hair Perfector pre-shampoo treatment and a sulfate-free shampoo. Slip is good, hair detangles cleanly under water; what I am still figuring out is whether the post-shower softness is the conditioner doing real bond work or just the silicones that came along for the ride.

Verdict, plus whether the cost-per-wash makes sense versus a cheaper salon brand, pending real notes.
