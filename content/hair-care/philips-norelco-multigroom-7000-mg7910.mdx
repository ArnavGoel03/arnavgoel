Philips's all-in-one Multigroom 7000, the 19-piece kit Amazon ships under the Norelco-branded SKU MG7910/49. One stainless steel handle, a full-size trimmer, a precision foil shaver, a detail trimmer, an extra-wide hair clipper head, and a nose-and-ear trimmer, all sharing the same lithium-ion body. Self-sharpening blades, no oil required.
