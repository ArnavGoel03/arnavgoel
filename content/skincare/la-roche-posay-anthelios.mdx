## Why I keep coming back

I went through a phase of trying mineral sunscreens because I wanted to feel virtuous about it. Every single one either pilled under my foundation or left me looking vaguely purple. Anthelios Melt-in is the one I always end up back at.

## Texture

It's closer to a lightweight moisturizer than a sunscreen. Two fingers' worth covers the face and neck with no heaviness and no shine after ten minutes.

## Performance

I wore this on a five-hour hike in Arizona in July. Reapplied twice. Zero burn, zero redness. That's the bar for me.

## Verdict

The only sunscreen in my closet right now.
