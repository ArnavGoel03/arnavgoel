## Why I picked this up

Retinal is the step up from retinol that K-beauty lines have been quietly leaning into, faster conversion to retinoic acid, less of the dry-papery week-three phase you get from a fresh retinol introduction. Beauty of Joseon's eye serum pairs it with ginseng, which the brand uses across the line as a low-irritation actives carrier.

## How I use it

Evening only. After serum, before moisturizer. A rice-grain dot on each side, patted around the orbital bone with the ring finger, never dragged.

## Early read

A few nights in. Full verdict after a month, retinal needs the runway before any honest read on tolerance and effect.
