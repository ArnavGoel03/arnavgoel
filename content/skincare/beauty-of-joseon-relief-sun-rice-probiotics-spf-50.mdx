## Why I bought it

Asian-formulated chemical sunscreens still set the bar for cosmetic elegance and Beauty of Joseon's Relief Sun is the version that gets recommended over and over for combination/oily skin without the white cast Western mineral sunscreens leave behind. Last step of the morning routine, every day. Two finger-lengths of product, warm between palms, press into the face and neck. Reapply at midday on long-outdoor days.
