## Why I bought it

Asian-formulated chemical sunscreens still set the bar for cosmetic elegance and Beauty of Joseon's Relief Sun is the version that gets recommended over and over for combination/oily skin without the white cast Western mineral sunscreens leave behind. Niacinamide and rice extract in the base do double duty.

## How I use it

Last step of the morning routine, every day. Two finger-lengths of product, warm between palms, press into the face and neck. Reapply at midday on long-outdoor days.

## Early read

The texture is the killer feature, light, slightly hydrating, no white cast, no separating under makeup. Full review after a month of daily wear.
