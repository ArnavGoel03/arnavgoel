## Why I use them

For years my strategy for a whitehead was "try not to pick it, fail, regret it for a week." Hydrocolloid patches short-circuit that loop. Stick one on before bed, wake up to a flat spot, no scab, no mark.

## How I use them

As soon as a pimple comes to a head, I clean and dry the skin around it, press a patch on, and leave it overnight. By morning the patch has turned milky-white, that's the fluid it pulled out, and the bump is either gone or close to it.

## What they don't do

These only work on surface-level whiteheads and small pustules. If it's a deep red cyst with no head, a patch won't touch it, the hydrocolloid needs fluid to absorb and there's nothing at the surface. Save them for what they're for.
