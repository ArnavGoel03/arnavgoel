## Why I bought it

I wanted a more concentrated ceramide step than my moisturizer was giving me, and the "ceramic" finish (a soft, smooth glow rather than a wet shine) sounded useful for days I wore base makeup.

## How I use it

Three drops after toner, before moisturizer. On no-makeup days that's the whole serum step. On makeup days it pulls double duty as a glow primer, base sits on top of it without separating.

## What it does well

The barrier story is the real one. After about a week of nightly use, the dry patches I sometimes get along the cheekbones smoothed out. The cosmetic glow effect is a nice bonus, not the main reason to buy.

## What it doesn't do

It's not a treatment serum (no actives like retinol, BHA, or vitamin C in any meaningful concentration). Pair it with a treatment, don't replace one.
