## Why I bought it

Needed a second-step foaming cleanser to follow the Beauty of Joseon balm without stripping the skin underneath. Caudalie has a reputation for fragrance-light formulas, and the grape-water base suggested something gentle enough for daily use on both routines.

## How I use it

Morning and evening. Pump a small amount into damp hands, work into a light lather, massage over the face for about thirty seconds, rinse. In the morning it is the only cleanse; in the evening it follows the balm as the water-based second step.

## What I am still figuring out

Whether the foam leaves the barrier feeling tight by week four, whether the bottle lasts long enough to justify a Sephora-only price in India, and how it reacts layered under the Anua TXA serum at night. Too early to call any of those.
