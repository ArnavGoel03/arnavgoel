## The pitch

Niacinamide is a legitimate active — it can reduce sebum, improve barrier function, and fade post-inflammatory marks. At $7, this was an obvious try.

## Six weeks in

My forehead was mildly less oily by week four. That's the positive column.

The negative column is longer: the serum pills under every moisturizer I paired it with, even ones specifically recommended as compatible. The first two weeks triggered breakouts along my jawline, which eventually settled but left marks.

## Would I recommend it?

Only if you're niacinamide-curious and don't want to spend $40 trying it. Otherwise, the Good Molecules version at the same price point layers much better.

## Verdict

Cheap, mildly effective, and annoying to use. Won't buy again.
