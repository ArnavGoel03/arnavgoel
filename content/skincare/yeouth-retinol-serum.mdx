## Why retinol at all

Retinol (and the rest of the retinoid family) has the strongest evidence base of any topical anti-aging active. Slow but consistent improvements in fine lines, texture, and post-inflammatory marks. The trade-off is the adjustment period, irritation, flaking, photosensitivity, which is why you ramp slowly.

## Ramp

Twice a week for the first two weeks. Three times a week for weeks three and four. Nightly only after my skin tolerated it without flaking. SPF the next morning is mandatory.

## Why I won't repurchase

Once I knew retinol worked for me, I'd rather move to a prescription tretinoin (much faster results) or to a more carefully formulated OTC retinoid like a granactive retinoid for slower skin. YEOUTH was a fine starter; I've graduated.
