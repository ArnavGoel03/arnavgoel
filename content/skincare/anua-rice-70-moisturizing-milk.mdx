## Why I bought it

I wanted a lightweight moisturizer that wouldn't pile under sunscreen but still left my skin actually hydrated, not just slick. Anua's rice line keeps showing up on K-beauty roundups and the formula list — niacinamide and ceramides on top of rice extract — was hard to fault.

## How I use it

Every morning after toner, before sunscreen. Two pumps for the face and neck. In the evening I'll layer it under a heavier cream on dry days, or use it on its own when my skin doesn't want anything thicker.

## What it does well

The "lotion" texture — somewhere between a milk and a thin cream — is the killer feature. It absorbs in seconds, doesn't pile under SPF, and the dewy finish reads as healthy skin rather than greasy skin. The ceramide content seems to do real work; I noticed less tightness around my cheeks within a couple of weeks.

## What it doesn't do

It's not a heavy occlusive. If your skin is properly dehydrated or it's deep winter, this layers under something heavier rather than replacing it.

## Verdict

The everyday moisturizer that earned its spot on the shelf.
