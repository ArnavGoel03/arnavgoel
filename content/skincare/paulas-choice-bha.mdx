## Context

I have combination skin that tilts oily on the nose and chin, and I've had persistent blackheads since puberty. I'd tried physical scrubs, clay masks, pore strips, and two other BHAs before landing here.

## Routine

Three nights a week, after cleansing, before moisturizer. I apply it with a cotton round across the T-zone and lightly across the cheeks. On sensitive-skin weeks I drop to twice a week.

## Results

The first week was unremarkable. Somewhere around day 10 I noticed my nose looked visibly smoother, and by day 21 the cluster of bumps along my forehead that I'd assumed were permanent had flattened out. Four months in, this is the closest thing to a "miracle product" I own.

## Caveats

You need sunscreen with this. Non-negotiable. BHAs increase photosensitivity and the whole point is to reveal fresh skin, which you will then promptly damage if you skip SPF.

## Verdict

I've repurchased this three times.
