## Why I bought it

I'd been doing single cleanses for years and my skin was paying for it, sunscreen residue on the pillowcase, clogged pores along the hairline. I wanted an oil-based first cleanse that wouldn't leave my face feeling coated. Beauty of Joseon kept coming up on every double-cleanse recommendation thread, so I picked up a tub from Nykaa.

## How I use it

Morning and evening, as the first step of a double cleanse. Scoop about a fingertip's worth, warm it between dry palms, massage over a dry face for thirty to sixty seconds, then add water to emulsify into a milky texture and rinse. Follow with a gentle water-based cleanser as the second step.

## What it does well

The emulsification is the thing. A lot of balms either stay oily or turn chalky when water hits them, this one turns into a clean milk and rinses without a film. Sunscreen, including heavy Asian sunscreens, comes off without me having to scrub.

## What it doesn't do

The jar is a minor annoyance. I keep a small spatula next to it, which solves the hygiene concern but adds a step. And the 100ml tub disappears in about two and a half months at nightly use, not expensive per wash, but you'll be reordering.
