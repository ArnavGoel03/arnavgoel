Stainless steel mini-blade with a guard, the heavier-weight alternative to the disposable Tinkle razors I had been using. Same purpose: monthly dermaplaning to clear peach fuzz and let actives sit closer to the skin. The heft of the steel handle is the difference; you can feel the angle the blade is taking, which is the entire reason a pro-grade dermaplaning blade beats a $4 plastic Tinkle.

## Routine

Once a month, on a clean dry face, alternated with the Ordinary peeling solution on a 15-day offset (so the skin barrier never compounds an acid burn with mechanical exfoliation). After the razor, hyaluronic acid plus an occlusive moisturizer. No actives that night.

Replace the blade every 3 to 4 sessions; the steel survives, the edge does not.
