## Why I picked this up

I wanted a foaming cleanser that didn't strip, and Innisfree's green-tea line keeps showing up on K-beauty shortlists for exactly that reason. The amino-acid surfactant base is gentler than the sulfate-driven foamers I've used before, and the larger 150 g tube is the right size for a cleanser I expect to use morning and night.

## How I use it

Pea-sized squeeze, lather between wet palms, work it across a damp face for thirty seconds, rinse. Mornings on its own, evenings as the second cleanser after an oil-based balm.

## Early read

The lather is dense without being squeaky, and my skin doesn't have that tight post-wash feeling I get from harsher gels. Full review after a month.
