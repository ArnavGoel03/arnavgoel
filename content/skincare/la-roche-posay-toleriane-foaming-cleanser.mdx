## Why I bought it

I needed a second cleanse to follow an oil-based balm, something that would actually foam, rinse fully, and not leave my cheeks tight. Most foaming cleansers over-strip to deliver a "squeaky clean" feel I've never wanted. Toleriane was the most recommended option that doesn't try to.

## How I use it

Every evening as the second cleanse. A single pump covers the face, lather for fifteen seconds, rinse with lukewarm water. Occasionally in the morning if my skin feels oily; otherwise I just rinse with water.

## What it does well

It foams without stripping, the best single sentence I can give it. The niacinamide and ceramides in the formula aren't just a marketing checkbox; my barrier feels intact after rinsing in a way it didn't with cheaper drugstore foams. pH sits in a reasonable range, no tight or squeaky feel afterward.

## What it doesn't do

It won't lift a heavy SPF day on its own, that's what the first cleanse is for. If you only ever do one step, use a cleansing balm instead and save this for days you go in lighter.
