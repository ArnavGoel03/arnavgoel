## Why I picked it up

I needed a thicker overnight lip product than the daytime balms I was rotating, something that actually sat on the lip and rebuilt the barrier overnight rather than wicking off after thirty minutes.

## How I use it

Last step of the night routine, after every other skincare layer has set. A generous swipe across the lips, leave it on while I sleep.

A few nights in. Verdict and routine notes after a month of consistent use.
