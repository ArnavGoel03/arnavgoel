## Why I picked it up

The Lip Sleeping Mask is the K-beauty lip product everyone keeps coming back to, dense balm in a small tub that sits on the lip overnight and rebuilds the barrier rather than wicking off in twenty minutes. The Gummy Bear edition is the same formula in a tinier tin — easier on the bedside table.

## How I use it

Last step of the night routine, after every other skincare layer has set. A generous fingertip swipe across both lips, leave it on while I sleep. The 8 g tin lasts a long time at one application a night.

## Still testing

A few nights in. Verdict and a side-by-side against the Lineage lip mask after a month.
