Replaced the Lasko box fan I returned in October. The Dreo Nomad One is a 25 ft/s velocity tower with 90 degree oscillation, 4 speeds, 4 modes (normal, natural, sleep, auto), an 8-hour timer, and a remote. Bladeless face plate, so it is safe to leave in a bedroom with the cat curious about it.

The single best feature is the sleep mode: it caps at the lowest two speeds and dims every LED on the unit to off (most fans leave at least the power LED bright enough to read by). The white-noise floor at speed 1 is almost identical to the white-noise machine I was running before, so I dropped the latter.

## What it replaces

A loud Lasko 20-inch box fan that ran one speed louder than I needed and had no timer. The tower form factor also frees up floor space the box fan blocked, which matters in a small bedroom.
