Lasko's 20-inch three-speed box fan, $21 at Target. The cheap-and-loud archetype. Returned after two weeks because the lowest speed was already louder than the running shower in the next room — fine if you genuinely want a fan as white noise, useless if you want airflow without acoustic intrusion.

For a quieter alternative under $40, the Vornado FA1-0118 sits in the same airflow class with a noticeably tighter motor. This stays off the shelf.
