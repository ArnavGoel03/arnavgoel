Anker's three-port GaN brick at $40, two USB-C and one USB-A, total 67W shared. Returned within two weeks. The split-power behaviour was the dealbreaker — plug a 30W phone in next to a 65W laptop and the laptop falls to slow-charge speeds because the brick has nowhere near enough headroom to actually deliver 65+30 simultaneously.

For a single-device wall plug this is fine. For the multi-device pitch the box sells, it does not have the watts. The Anker 65W Nano III at $35 does single-port charging just as well in a smaller body; the older 100W four-port brick is the actual upgrade if you really need to charge a laptop and a phone at the same time.
