WHOOP Peak is the screenless 24/7 strap that bills sleep, HRV, RHR, strain, and recovery into one daily score. The Peak tier (vs ONE / Life) adds healthspan, stress monitoring, and the new heart-screening features. 14+ days of battery on the 5.0, charged via the slide-on battery pack so you never take the strap off.

Verdict pending the first quarterly report. The signal I am watching for is whether WHOOP's recovery score actually predicts how a workout will feel, vs. just retroactively narrating it. Reviewing this in earnest at the 90-day mark when the algorithm has enough nights of sleep data to mean something.
