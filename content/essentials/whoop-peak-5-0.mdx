WHOOP Peak is the screenless 24/7 strap that bills sleep, HRV, RHR, strain, and recovery into one daily score. Peak (vs ONE / Life) adds healthspan, stress monitoring, and the new heart-screening features. 14+ days of battery on the 5.0, charged via the slide-on battery pack so you never take the strap off.

The single feature that earns the daily wear is the recovery score: the algorithm weights HRV, sleep performance, and resting HR into one number that has consistently predicted how a workout will feel before I get to the gym. Days when WHOOP says recovery is 30%, the warm-up sets feel heavier; days at 90%, PRs come easier. That is the loop I am buying when I keep this strap on every day, including in the shower and through sleep.

The trade-off is the subscription model: the device is "free" with the 12-month membership but the membership renews. Compared to the Apple Watch I rotate off when I want a screenless night, WHOOP is the one I keep on. No notifications, no glance temptation, just the strap and the morning score.
