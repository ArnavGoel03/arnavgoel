Steripod clip-on toothbrush cover, two-pack, the thymol-vapour pods that snap onto a brush head and supposedly keep airborne bathroom mist off the bristles between brushes. Trial size lasts about three months per pod by the manufacturer's claim.

The thymol claim is hard to verify — most bathroom contamination on a toothbrush comes from your own mouth, not the air, so the bigger value here is the physical cover during travel. Verdict on whether the pod actually loses its scent on schedule (the only way to tell when to replace), plus whether the clip stays tight on a Sonicare-style head, pending real notes.
