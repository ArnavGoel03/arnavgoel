CrossAction is the angled-bristle replacement head for any of Oral-B's round-head electric handles (Pro 1000 through iO series). The 16-degree bristle angle is the marginal improvement over the FlossAction and Precision Clean heads I have rotated through; the angle catches the gumline crevices that flat-bristle heads skim.

## Routine

Swap every 12 weeks per ADA guidance. Going past 16 weeks visibly frays the bristles and the cleaning falls off a cliff (the dentist will tell you this on your next checkup). A 4-pack lasts a year on a single-handle household.

The price gap between OEM Oral-B heads and the off-brand "compatible" ones on Amazon is real but smaller than it looks: the off-brand bristles do not ride the handle's pulse and oscillate cycles cleanly, which is most of why the OEM cleaning feels different.
