Stannous-fluoride paste from the Crest Pro-Health line, marketed for gum health rather than whitening. I rotate it in when my gums feel a little inflamed; the stannous side does seem to settle that down faster than a sodium-fluoride-only paste, though the trade-off is that stannous can stain over time, which is why this rotates rather than living in the cup full-time.
