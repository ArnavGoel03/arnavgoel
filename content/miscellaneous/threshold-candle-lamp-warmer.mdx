Target's house-brand candle warmer, $30 frosted-globe lamp that uses a halogen bulb to melt a candle from above instead of a wick burning it from inside. The pitch is a flame-free way to throw scent — you get the diffusion of a burning candle without smoke, soot, or having to remember to blow it out.

Verdict pending real time with a few candle pours: how the heat treats softer waxes (Bath & Body Works tends to over-pool), how loud the bulb is at desk distance, and whether the throw actually reaches across a room or stays in the immediate radius.
