Anodized aluminium round pill case, the puck-style organizer with a screw-on lid and a single chamber. Holds a day's stack (creatine pill form when I am traveling, fish oil, ashwagandha, vitamin D) without a divider, so the case is ergonomic in a coat pocket and survives a backpack drop without rattling.

Beats the multi-day plastic Sun-Mon-Tue boxes for travel because there is no wasted volume. The screw lid stays sealed in checked luggage; the rubber gasket keeps powder leakage out of the case if a capsule splits.
