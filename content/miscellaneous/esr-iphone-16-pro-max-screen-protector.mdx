Three-pack of 9H tempered glass with the alignment frame ESR ships in their high-volume packs. Frame-assisted install means there is no margin for crooked placement; you drop the phone in, peel the protector, the frame snaps it on dust-free.

Glass clarity is identical to the bare display, no rainbow-banding under daylight, no Face ID degradation. Oleophobic coating wears off after about three months of daily use, which is why ESR ships three at this price point: rotate one in every quarter and the phone face stays effectively scratch-free for 9 months on one purchase.

The protectors do not interfere with iPhone 16 Pro Max cases, including the slim Apple silicone case I have been alternating with the Apple FineWoven.
