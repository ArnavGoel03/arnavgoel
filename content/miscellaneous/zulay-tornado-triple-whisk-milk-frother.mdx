Battery-powered handheld frother with three whisk attachments: single-coil for milk, double-coil for thicker foam, and a flat blender disc for matcha and pre-workouts. The triple-head pack is what makes the $17 version of this category interesting; a $5 generic frother only ships with one head and clogs on matcha.

The single-coil whisk turns 2% milk into stiff foam in 20 seconds. The matcha disc breaks up clumps fast enough that I stopped sieving. AAA batteries (4 included) last about a month of daily use.

## Routine

One pull a day for matcha, two on weekends when I add cold-foam to cold brew. Replaces a $200 espresso-machine steam wand for the tier of foam I actually need at home.
