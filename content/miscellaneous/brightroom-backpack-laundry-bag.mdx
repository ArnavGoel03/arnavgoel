Brightroom's drawstring laundry bag with backpack straps, $12 at Target. Textured gray polyester, holds about a week of clothes for one person. The straps are the whole point: a hamper bag that walks itself to the laundry room without lopsiding the shoulder.

Three months in and the seams have not given. The backpack format is the difference between dragging a half-full hamper through dorm hallways and just wearing the laundry on my back. For $12 it is the highest-utility item in the apartment.
