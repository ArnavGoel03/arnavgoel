Battery-powered fabric shaver, the small handheld kind with a domed mesh head and a rotating blade behind it. Pulls pilling off knit sweaters, fleece, and cotton tees with the kind of obvious before-after that makes you do every garment in the closet in one sitting.

The reason I keep this in rotation: a pilled merino sweater goes from "donate it" to "wear it again" in 90 seconds. Spend $15 once, save the cost of replacing every winter knit. Empties the lint chamber three or four times per sweater on the first pass.

Battery life is the only weak point: AA batteries (not built-in lithium), which means it dies mid-job if you forget to swap. Keeps a spare set in the drawer.
