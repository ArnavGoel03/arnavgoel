Doublewide wristbands, two per pack (one black, one grey), Dri-Fit terry. Used on the racket-side wrist for tennis and on heavy-deadlift days to keep sweat off the chalk. The doublewide thickness is the key choice; the standard width is too narrow to hold a real workout's sweat without saturating in the first set.

These specific Nike bands keep their elastic for years; the cheaper Amazon-basic alternatives go limp after a month of washing. One pair in rotation, washed weekly with the gym laundry load.
