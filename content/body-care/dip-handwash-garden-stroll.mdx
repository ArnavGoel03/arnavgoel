## Why I picked it up

DIP (Dream. Immerse. Play.) is a fragrance-led Indian body line that puts most of the design budget into the bottle and the scent profile, which is the right call for hand soap — it sits on the counter all day, so the tactile and olfactory experience matters more than active claims.

## How I use it

Counter pump next to the basin. One press, lather, rinse. Garden Stroll is the green-floral variant in the line.

## Still testing

A few weeks in. Update on whether the formula dries the hands by week three, and whether the scent fades fast enough to disappear before the next thing I touch is dinner.
