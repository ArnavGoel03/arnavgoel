Manscaped's facial counterpart to its body trimmer line, sold around $100. Returned. The blades cut fine for the first week, but next to the Philips Norelco Multigroom 7000 sitting on the same shelf at the same price, it never had a real reason to stay — the Philips includes 19 attachments and a body trimmer, this is one head with a bunch of guards.

The brand leans hard on the "SkinSafe" name and the influencer ecosystem around it. That's the price you're paying. For a beard trimmer at the $100 tier, the Philips wins on both attachments-per-dollar and runtime, which is why this went back to Target inside the return window. Verdict: bad value, not bad hardware.
